## Our pledge

We are committed to making the Web Dev & Design community a welcoming place for everyone, regardless of experience level, background, or identity.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in discussions
- Give and accept feedback graciously
- Help others learn without gatekeeping
- Credit sources and respect intellectual property
- Use inclusive language

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Spam, self-promotion outside designated channels, or scams
- Sharing others' private information without consent
- Deliberately derailing conversations or trolling

## Enforcement

Moderators may warn, mute, or remove members who violate these guidelines. Serious or repeated violations may result in a permanent ban.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness a violation, contact a moderator in Discord or reach out through our community channels.

## Attribution

This code of conduct is adapted from common open-source community guidelines and tailored for our Discord server.
