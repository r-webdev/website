description: Community guidelines for Web Dev & Design on Discord.

We are committed to making the Web Dev & Design community a welcoming place for everyone. These rules apply to all members and must be followed at all times.

## Community Rules

1. **Be Respectful:** No bullying, insults, or discrimination. Respect pronouns; homophobia and transphobia are strictly prohibited and will result in a ban.
2. **Follow Discord ToS:** No piracy, game cheats, or hacking discussions.
3. **English Only:** All communication must be in English to allow for effective moderation.
4. **No NSFW:** Explicit content is strictly prohibited.
5. **Channel Etiquette:** Use the correct channels. Do not cross-post, spam, send invite links, or beg for help.
6. **No Promo or Hiring:** Commercial promotion, recruitment, freelance gigs, and seeking paid/unpaid workers are forbidden.

   Asking for feedback is allowed as long as it does not violate the rules above.

7. **No Unsolicited DMs:** Get explicit permission before DMing a user. Do not DM users to bypass rules or trade help for private messages. Keep help public.
8. **Protect Privacy:** Do not share the personal information of other users.
9. **Appropriate Usernames:** Use clean, easy-to-tag names without advertisements, impersonations, or offensive terms.
10. **Report Problems:** Use the [ticket system](https://discord.com/channels/434487340535382016/1384222210822111302) for issues.
11. **No Academic Dishonesty:** We will help you understand your homework, but we will not do it for you.
12. **Share Code Properly:** Do not upload raw code files or post shortened URLs (TypeScript Playground links are the only exception). Use third-party sandboxes—in Discord, run `/tips subject:Sandbox` for recommended options.
13. **No Web3 or Gambling:** Discussions, links, or solicitations regarding crypto, NFTs, blockchain, and gambling are completely banned.

## Enforcement

Moderators may warn, mute, or remove members who violate these guidelines. Serious or repeated violations—including homophobia, transphobia, and other severe misconduct—may result in a permanent ban.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness a violation, use the [ticket system](https://discord.com/channels/434487340535382016/1384222210822111302) or contact a moderator in Discord.
